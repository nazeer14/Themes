Our Story

Building Bright Futures
Since 1999

Luminary Academy was founded on a singular belief — that every child possesses extraordinary potential. Over 26 years, we've built an environment where academic rigour meets creative freedom, producing graduates who lead industries worldwide.

Our 180+ passionate educators combine time-tested pedagogy with 21st-century innovation, ensuring every student receives a world-class, deeply personalised education.
