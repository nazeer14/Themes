About AroraCare

Healing With Heart & Science

Founded in 2009, AroraCare Clinic has been a cornerstone of community health in our city. We combine cutting-edge medical technology with warm, personalized care to help every patient live their healthiest life.

Our mission is simple: to deliver exceptional, affordable healthcare with integrity and compassion. We believe every patient deserves respectful attention, transparent communication, and the very best in medical expertise.

Compassion
We treat every patient like family, with dignity and care.
Innovation
Advanced diagnostics and evidence-based treatment plans.
Integrity
Honest, transparent care you can always rely on.
Community
Dedicated to improving health outcomes for everyone.
What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

From routine check-ups to advanced specialist care — we cover every aspect of your health under one roof.

Cardiology
Expert heart care including ECGs, stress tests, and cardiac consultations for all age groups.
Neurology
Diagnosis and treatment for neurological conditions, from headaches to complex neurological disorders.
Orthopedics
Joint, spine, and sports injury care with minimally invasive surgical options available.
Pediatrics
Compassionate care for children from newborns to teenagers, covering all developmental milestones.
Dental Care
Full dental services from preventive cleanings to cosmetic and restorative procedures.
Ophthalmology

Pulmonology
Respiratory care for asthma, COPD, and other lung conditions using modern diagnostics.
Laboratory & Diagnostics
Comprehensive blood work, imaging, and pathology services with fast turnaround times.
